I come from India, from a small town called Mahe in the state of Kerala. I have visited many states and I would say my most favorite one would be New Delhi when I visited the Taj Mahal, it was one of the most beautiful buildings I have ever seen.

I have a lot of relatives living in Malaysia, and i visit them at least once a year. I'd say Kuala lumpur would be my favorite destination due to it's beautiful city skyline and the Petronas towers! The people are so polite and welcoming.

Switzerland would be the best country I have visited yet. The chilly weather during winter is so magnificent. The best place I have seen so far in Switzerland would be Mount Titlis. It was a great walk up there with my family and we all a had really good time.
